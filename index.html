Welcome to Work in Progress! It is a brand new student podcast that is interested in the way design students talk about their thesis projects. Each episode is hosted by Karissa Munaf, a senior at Parsons School of Design. We will talk about the subject matter but particularly on the research and stories that students found in their theses.
